18 December 2021

Anonmare updated:

  • The AI can now speak the languages of the crew (except the ones that it would not logically be able to).
  • Capascin provide nutrition, on par with soy sauce. Some nutriments are now more pleasing than others.
  • Drinks now provide sanity benefit. Salt and pepper are *ever so* slightly good for your sanity now. Poisonberry juice and wine is 4x better for sanity as their counterparts, if you're willing to bear the side effects.

Hatterhat updated:

  • Crafting stations should now, theoretically, drop their loaded materials when deconstructed.
  • Hydraulic circuits have returned to increasing tool speed, and not coating tools in plasma.

HentaiStorm updated:

  • added missing watterbottle icons
  • Add halberd back icon

Humonitarian updated:

  • tables and low walls protect the leg instead of having a random chance to eat bullets
  • refactored the targeting check of bullets that updates the targeted limb when hitting a mob
  • resting now doesn't force you to drop items
  • you can manage your inventory while resting, but not use them to interact with the world

Iglpigl updated:

  • origin descriptions that give you a language say so now so you don't need to guess
  • Adds old rockets to the things you can build on the crafting station; Re-added AMR and RPG revolvers with correct numbers
  • Removed plastic cap revolver from possible revolvers artist can make
  • tweaked recoil, damage, AP, one hand penalty of all artist revolvers to take into account recoil rework and other variable changes that happened in the meantime
  • Fixes minor spelling mistake when crafting station was full, also makes normal RPG ammo description more organic

  • Removed Jester and Warrior causing natural maintenance to crew area migrations due to problems with implementation.
